3rd and Final Hackagong meetup – Thurs 6:30pm @ The Brewery

  • 3rd and Final Hackagong meetup – Thurs 6:30pm @ The Brewery

on Jul 14 2013 by Hackagong com 0 cat News

With over 100 tickets sold, and only 5 days until the epic Hackagong kicks off in Wollongong, we’re hosting our 3rd and final casual meetup this Thursday 18th July at 6:30pm @ the Illawarra Brewery.

If you’re still looking for team members for Hackagong, have any questions, or just simply want to hangout with some really cool people and talk geek, then come along.

Our previous meetups attracted around 20-30 people each, with many new teams formed, including lots of discussions about project ideas and 3D printing applications.

RSVP on the facebook event: Hackagong FINAL Casual Meetup #3: Meet Potential Team Members!
